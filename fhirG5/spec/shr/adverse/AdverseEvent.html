AdverseEvent [Entry]

Any unfavorable and unintended sign, symptom, disease, or other medical occurrence with a temporal association with the use of a medical product, procedure or other therapy, or in conjunction with a research study, regardless of causal relationship. EXAMPLE(S): death, back pain, headache, pulmonary embolism, heart attack. C0877248
