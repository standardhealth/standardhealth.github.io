FocalSubject

The person or thing that this entry refers to, usually the Person of Record. However, not all entries refer to the Person of Record. The entry could refer to a fetus, care giver, or relative (living or dead).
Value: Choice
        | reference to PersonOfRecord 1 Extended demographic information about the subject of this SHR.
        | reference to Practitioner 1 A person who practices a healing art.
        | reference to RelatedPerson 1 A person, other than a practitioner or the patient (the person of record) who is relevant to the health or social situation of the subject.
