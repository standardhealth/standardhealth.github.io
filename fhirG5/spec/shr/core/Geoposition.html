Geoposition

The location on the surface of the Earth, described by a latitude and longitude (and optional altitude).
Latitude 1 The angular distance north or south between an imaginary line around a heavenly body parallel to its equator and the equator itself. Measured with with WGS84 datum.
Longitude 1 An imaginary great circle on the surface of a heavenly body passing through the poles at right angles to the equator. Measured with with WGS84 datum.
Altitude optional Height above sea level or above the earth's surface. Measured with with WGS84 datum.
