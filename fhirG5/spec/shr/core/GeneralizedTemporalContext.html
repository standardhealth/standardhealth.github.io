GeneralizedTemporalContext

A generalized indicator of a dateTime or an age when an event happened.
Value: Choice
        | GeneralizedDateTime 1 A point in time, specified qualitatively or quantitatively.
        | GeneralizedAge 1 Qualitative or quantitative, exact or inexact description of age.
        | GestationalTemporalContext 1 A named gestational time period, or a gestational age.
