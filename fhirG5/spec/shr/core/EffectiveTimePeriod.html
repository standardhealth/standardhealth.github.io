EffectiveTimePeriod

The date and time span for which something is active, valid, or in force. C2985763
Based On: TimePeriod A period of time defined by a start and end time, date, or year.
TimePeriodStart 1 The time at which something is to take effect, start, or did start.
TimePeriodEnd optional The time at which something is to end or did end.
