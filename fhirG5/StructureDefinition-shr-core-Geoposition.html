SHR Geoposition Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-core-Geoposition

The location on the surface of the Earth, described by a latitude and longitude (and optional altitude).

This profile builds on Location.

This profile was published on Mon Jul 31 23:41:40 EDT 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.

SHR Mapping Source

This structure represents the following SHR mapping definition:

shr.core.Geoposition maps to Location:
  constrain position to 1..1
  Latitude maps to position.latitude
  Longitude maps to position.longitude
  Altitude maps to position.altitude
