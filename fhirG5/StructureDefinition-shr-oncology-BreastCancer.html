SHR BreastCancer Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-oncology-BreastCancer

A cancer originating in the tissues of the breast, and potentially spread to other organs of the body.

This profile builds on Condition.
