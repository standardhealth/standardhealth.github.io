SHR RelatedPerson Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-actor-RelatedPerson

A person, other than a practitioner or the patient (the person of record) who is relevant to the health or social situation of the subject.

This profile builds on RelatedPerson.

This profile was published on Mon Jul 31 22:25:17 EDT 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
