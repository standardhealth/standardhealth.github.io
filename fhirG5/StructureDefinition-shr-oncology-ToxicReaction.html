SHR ToxicReaction Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-oncology-ToxicReaction

An adverse reaction to medication, radiation treatment, or other therapy, that causes grade 3 or 4 adverse reaction.

This profile builds on AdverseEvent.

This profile was published on Mon Jul 31 19:48:46 EDT 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
