SHR Medication Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-medication-Medication

A type of prescription drug or over-the-counter drug that is used to prevent, treat, or relieve symptoms of a disease or abnormal condition, but excluding vaccines.

This profile builds on Medication.

This profile was published on Mon Jul 31 19:48:41 EDT 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
