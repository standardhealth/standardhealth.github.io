  <p><b>SHR Progression Profile</b></p>
  <p>A finding related to the current trend of a disease. This concept is most often used for chronic and incurable diseases where the status and trendline of the disease is an important determinant of therapy and prognosis. The specific disorder being evaluated must be cited in the AssessmentFocus as a reference to a Condition.</p>
  <p><b>SHR Mapping Summary</b></p>
  <p><pre>shr.oncology.Progression maps to ClinicalImpression:
  Entry.FocalSubject maps to subject
  Entry.AssociatedEncounter maps to context 
  Entry.Informant maps to assessor
  Entry.OriginalCreationDate maps to date
  Status maps to status
  SpecificType maps to code
  ClinicallyRelevantTime.GeneralizedTemporalContext.GeneralizedDateTime.TimePeriod maps to effective[x].Period
  ClinicallyRelevantTime.GeneralizedTemporalContext.GeneralizedDateTime.dateTime maps to effective[x].dateTime
  Summary maps to summary
  AssessmentResult.CodeableConcept maps to finding.item[x].CodeableConcept
  Evidence maps to investigation.item 
</pre></p>
