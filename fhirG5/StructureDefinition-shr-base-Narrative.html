SHR Narrative Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-base-Narrative

A human-readable narrative, potentially including images, that contains a summary of the resource, and may be used to represent the content of the resource to a human.

This profile builds on Narrative.

This profile was published on Mon Jul 31 22:25:17 EDT 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
