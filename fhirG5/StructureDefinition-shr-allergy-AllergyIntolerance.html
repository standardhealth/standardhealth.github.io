SHR AllergyIntolerance Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-allergy-AllergyIntolerance

A statement pertaining to an individual's allergies and intolerances to substances or classes of substances. Can be used to record that a substance does not pose an elevated risk to the subject (AssertionNegationModifier = true).

This profile builds on AllergyIntolerance.
