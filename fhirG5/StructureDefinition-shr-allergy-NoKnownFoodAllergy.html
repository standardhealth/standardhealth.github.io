SHR NoKnownFoodAllergy Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-allergy-NoKnownFoodAllergy

The subject has no known allergies to any foods.

This profile builds on AllergyIntolerance.
