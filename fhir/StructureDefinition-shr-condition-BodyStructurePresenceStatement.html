BodyStructurePresenceStatementProfile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-condition-BodyStructurePresenceStatement

Presence of a distinct anatomical or pathological morphological feature or organizational pattern, acquired or innate. Examples include tissue types, tumors, and wounds. Body structures are continuants that allow observations of the same body structure to be related to be tracked over time.

This profile builds on US Core Condition (a.k.a Problem) Profile.

This profile was published on Wed Aug 22 00:00:00 EDT 2018 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
