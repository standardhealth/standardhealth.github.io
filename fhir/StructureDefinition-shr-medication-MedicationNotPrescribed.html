SHR MedicationNotPrescribed Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-medication-MedicationNotPrescribed

A record of a medication NOT prescribed. Recorded only when deviating from the normal expectation, care plan, or standard of care.

This profile builds on MedicationRequest.

This profile was published on Wed May 10 14:00:16 EDT 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
