SHR Injury Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-condition-Injury

Damage inflicted on the body by an external force.

This profile builds on US Core Condition (a.k.a Problem) Profile.
