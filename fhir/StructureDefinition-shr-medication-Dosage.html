SHR Dosage Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-medication-Dosage

The dosage of the medication as prescribed. Do not include if dosage was not known.

This profile builds on Dosage.

This profile was published on Tue Dec 12 00:00:00 EST 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
