SHR Encounter Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-encounter-Encounter

A statement about a planned or actual interaction between a patient and healthcare provider(s) for the purpose of providing healthcare service(s) or assessing the health status of a patient.

This profile builds on Encounter.

This profile was published on Tue Aug 08 11:56:32 EDT 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.

SHR Mapping Source

This structure represents the following SHR mapping definition:

shr.encounter.Encounter maps to Encounter:
  Entry.FocalSubject maps to subject
  Status maps to status
  OccurrenceTime maps to period
  EncounterClass maps to class
  ServiceGiven maps to type
  Participant maps to participant.individual
  Participant.OccurrenceTime maps to participant.period
  Participant.ParticipationType maps to participant.type
  ProviderOrganization maps to serviceProvider
