SHR SupportSurface Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-skin-SupportSurface

A specific instance of a support surface used to distribute pressure and support a patient. The value is coding of the type of support surface.

This profile builds on Device.

This profile was published on Thu Feb 23 16:26:39 EST 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
