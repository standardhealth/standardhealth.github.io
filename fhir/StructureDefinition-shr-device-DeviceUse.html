SHR DeviceUse Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-device-DeviceUse

A statement pertaining to the use of a device by a subject. May document the use of a device, or that the subject does not use a particular type of device.

This profile builds on DeviceUseStatement.

This profile was published on Wed May 10 14:00:16 EDT 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
