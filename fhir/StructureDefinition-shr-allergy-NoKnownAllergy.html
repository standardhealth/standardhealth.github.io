SHR NoKnownAllergy Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-allergy-NoKnownAllergy

The subject has no known allergies to drugs, foods, biologics, or environmental factors.

This profile builds on AllergyIntolerance.

This profile was published on Thu Feb 23 16:26:39 EST 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
