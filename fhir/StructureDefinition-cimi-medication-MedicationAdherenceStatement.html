MedicationAdherenceStatementProfile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/cimi-medication-MedicationAdherenceStatement

The degree (frequency) that the stated treatment plan, prescription, or protocol was followed. A statement of the ability and cooperation of the patient in taking medicine or supplement as recommended or prescribed. This includes correct timing, dosage, and frequency.

This profile builds on US Core Results Profile.

This profile was published on Wed Aug 22 00:00:00 EDT 2018 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
