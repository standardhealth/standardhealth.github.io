SubstanceProfile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/cimi-entity-Substance

Any matter of defined composition that has discrete existence, whose origin may be biological, mineral or chemical.

This profile builds on Substance.

This profile was published on Tue Aug 21 00:00:00 EDT 2018 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
