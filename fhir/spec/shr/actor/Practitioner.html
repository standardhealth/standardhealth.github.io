Practitioner

A person who practices a healing art. C2220264
Based On: Person A person. In practical terms, people relevant to the health or social situation of the subject (including the person of record).
Value: HumanName A name used by a human being, written as it would be typically expressed. May include a breakdown of the various elements of the name (family name, given name, etc.).
