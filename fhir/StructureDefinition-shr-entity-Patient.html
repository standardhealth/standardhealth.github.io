SHR Patient Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-entity-Patient

A person in the role of a patient, including extended demographic information about the subject of this health record.

This profile builds on US Core Patient Profile.

This profile was published on Tue Dec 12 00:00:00 EST 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
