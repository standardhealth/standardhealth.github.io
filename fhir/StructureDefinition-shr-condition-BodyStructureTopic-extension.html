BodyStructureTopicExtension

 

The official URL for this extension is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-condition-BodyStructureTopic-extension

Presence (or absence) of a distinct anatomical or pathological morphological feature or organizational pattern, acquired or innate. Examples include tissue types, tumors, and wounds.

This extension was published on Tue Aug 21 00:00:00 EDT 2018 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
