SHR GeopoliticalLocation Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-core-GeopoliticalLocation

The countries of the world and major geopolitical subregions, such as US states.

This profile builds on Address.

This profile was published on Tue Aug 08 11:56:20 EDT 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.

SHR Mapping Source

This structure represents the following SHR mapping definition:

shr.core.GeopoliticalLocation maps to Address:
  AddressLine maps to line
  City maps to city
  County maps to district
  State maps to state
  PostalCode maps to postalCode
  Country maps to country
  constrain use to 0..0
  constrain type to 0..0
