SHR UpperBound Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-core-UpperBound

A measured or measurable amount with units that is known to be bounded from above by the quantity represented.

This profile builds on Quantity.

This profile was published on Tue May 09 14:19:24 EDT 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
