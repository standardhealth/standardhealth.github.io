The Standard Health Record Data Elements

The Standard Health Record is a rapidly evolving specification under active development. Ultimately, an individual's SHR will consist of a series of entries relevant to the health of that person. The entries are divided among the categories below, and each entry always includes basic information about itself (e.g. who created it and when). Click on the data elements below to explore details of the Standard Health Record. Go to the SHR GitHub or email the collaborative to get involved.
