QualitativeLikelihoodVS

The likelihood of an event or state, expressed qualitatively. Qualitative likelihoods are associated with judgments and assessments. Values are based on US Intelligence Community's standard language used to describe judgements of likelihood.

Code Display CodeSystem
definitely With certainty or near-complete certainty, an event that definitely has or almost certainly will happen QualitativeLikelihoodCS
very_likely Very likely, highly probable, e.g., an event with more than 80% but less than 100% likelihood QualitativeLikelihoodCS
likely Likely or probable, e.g., an event with 60% to 80% likelihood QualitativeLikelihoodCS
even_chance Roughly equal chance or even odds of happening, between 40% and 60% probability QualitativeLikelihoodCS
unlikely Not likely or improbable, e.g., an event with 20% to 40% likelihood QualitativeLikelihoodCS
very_unlikely Very unlikely, highly improbable, e.g., an event with less than 20% but non-zero likelihood QualitativeLikelihoodCS
